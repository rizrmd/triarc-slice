# VanGambit — Gameplay Overview

## What Is It?

A real-time auto-battler where heroes fight on their own. Your job is strategic — pick the right heroes, manage energy, drag action cards onto heroes to empower them, and assign targets. Think coaching a squad, not controlling individuals.

## Flow

1. **Sign in** with Google
2. **Pick 3 heroes** from a roster of 17 (selection persists between sessions)
3. **Queue** for PvP matchmaking or start a training match vs AI
4. **Battle** in real-time until one team is eliminated

## The Match

Your 3 heroes face 3 enemy heroes on screen simultaneously. Heroes fight autonomously — they have their own skills, passives, reactions, and ultimates that fire without your input.

### What You Control

**Action Cards** — You're dealt a hand of cards (Fireball, Frostbolt, Shield Wall, etc.). Each has an energy cost (1–4). Drag a card onto one of your heroes to cast it. The hero becomes the caster and the server resolves the target. The same card plays differently depending on which hero casts it — a Fireball through Flame Warlock is faster and spreads ignite; through Frost Queen it's slower but might create steam.

**Energy** — Your team shares an energy pool (starts at 10). Spend it wisely across your cards.

**Reroll** — Swap your hand for new cards. Using all cards in your hand triggers a free reroll automatically.

**Targeting** — Each hero auto-picks primary and secondary enemy targets at match start. You can reassign targets at any time.

### What Heroes Control

**Skills** — Fire on cooldowns (7–20s). Basic attacks, AoE blasts, heals.

**Passives** — Always active. Examples: "+10% defense to adjacent allies", "on damage dealt, apply burn".

**Reactions** — Trigger on game events: damage taken, ally death, HP dropping below a threshold, etc. Some have a chance to fire, some fire only once per match.

**Ultimates** — Charge through specific methods (damage dealt, kills, healing done, time, etc.) and fire automatically when the threshold is reached. Match-defining moments.

### Hero Personality

Every ability has a "thought" — a short line shown when it activates, like "EVERYTHING BURNS!" or "If I fall... you ALL burn with me!" This makes the autonomous behavior feel characterful rather than mechanical.

## Hero Roster

17 heroes across 6 elements (fire, ice, earth, wind, light, shadow) filling roles from glass cannon to anchor tank to healer. Heroes have alignment compatibility — some synergize, some conflict. Team composition matters.

## Match End

When one team's heroes are all eliminated, the match ends. You return to either the home screen or hero select (training mode).
