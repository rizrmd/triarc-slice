# Game Pane Plan: Bulwark

**Hero:** Garen, the Iron Knight

## Unique Concept

Bulwark operates on a classic tank archetype: Garen absorbs pressure from enemies and allies alike, converting incoming attacks and defensive actions into a resource that can be spent to protect his team.

Unlike single-target damage dealers who seek to eliminate threats quickly, Balwark turns defensive pressure into offensive advantage. The more damage Garen takes or the more cards he uses to defend, the more powerful he becomes at shielding allies and redirecting critical hits.

## Resource System

### Defense Stack

**How it Accumulates:**

- Enemy attacks that hit Garen directly
- Defensive cards that Garen plays
- Successful ally protection actions

**Defense Stack Functions:**

- Strengthens Garen's armor
- Grants shields to single allies or the entire team
- Activates redirect effects that transfer damage from allies to Garen

## Gameplay Impact

**Player Visibility:**

- Current Defense Stack count
- Available defensive skills
- Skills that have sufficient stack to activate

**Strategic Decisions:**

1. **Immediate Defense:** Spend Defense Stack now to shield a weak ally
2. **Redirect Activation:** Enable protective mode so next target switches from ally to Garen
3. **Stack Hoarding:** Accumulate more Defense Stack for bigger team shields later
4. **Redirect Priority:** Use redirect on high-threat enemies to protect carries

## Core Mechanics

- **Absorbing Phase:** Garen gains Defense Stack by taking damage and defending
- **Spending Phase:** Defense Stack converts into armor upgrades, shields, or redirects
- **Redirect Mechanic:** Incoming attacks to allies can be redirected to Garen, who tank's them better
- **Team Protection:** Stack spent across entire team for area shields

## Design Principles

- Pressure converts to power: more damage taken = more resources to defend team
- Defensive investment becomes offensive capability at key moments
- Redirect mechanics turn enemy focus into advantage
- Stack system prevents Garen from being a passive damage sponge
- Multiple spending options give player control over timing

**Summary:** Bulwark transforms Garen from a simple target-absorber into an ultimate protector who turns enemy aggression into team shields and defensive capability. Each attack Garen takes brings him closer to overwhelming team protection.
