# Gunslinger — Renzo

## Identity

- **Name:** Renzo
- **Role:** Burst DPS
- **Alignment:** Neutral
- **Elements:** Fire (+20), Wind (+15), Light (+10)
- **Weaknesses:** Earth (-15), Ice (-10), Shadow (-5)
- **Stats:** 2500 HP / 165 ATK / 105 DEF

## Lore

Renzo is a freelance marksman who built his own weapons — fire-charged shells propelled by compressed wind. In a world of swords and sorcery, he chose explosions and velocity. Most mages considered it crude. Most mages are dead now.

He grew up in the trade cities — no clan, no order, no bloodline. Just a kid who figured out that fire and wind, channeled through a steel barrel, could outrun any spell. He took his first contract at sixteen. By twenty, he had a reputation. By twenty-five, he had a price on his head in three kingdoms and a waiting list of clients in twelve more.

He doesn't fight for causes. Causes get people killed for free. He fights for coin, for the challenge, and for the look on a mage's face when they realize a bullet is faster than an incantation. He's not cruel — he's professional. The job gets done, the payment arrives, and Renzo moves on to the next city.

He competes with Seryn on accuracy and with Kaelen on speed. He loses to both in their specialties and beats both in versatility. He's the only fighter on the field who can hit anything, anywhere, at any range, without magic — and he'll never let you forget it.

## Personality

Cocky, fast-talking, irreverent. Renzo treats combat like a performance and every kill like a punchline. He's the loudest person on any battlefield and the least bothered by the consequences. Underneath the swagger: sharp instincts, genuine skill, and a complete lack of sentimentality. He doesn't form attachments because attachments are liabilities. He has colleagues, not friends. Clients, not allies.

The only thing he takes seriously is the craft. A missed shot offends him more than a betrayal.

### Quick Shot
The fastest cast in the game — 500ms. A single precise shot at his primary target. Not the hardest hit, but the quickest. Renzo believes the first shot wins, and he's usually right.
- *Thought: "Bang."*

### Explosive Round
A fire-charged shell that detonates on impact, splashing damage to nearby enemies. His bread-and-butter for dealing with groups — hit one, hurt everyone standing near them.
- *Thought: "Try dodging this!"*

### Fan the Hammer
Renzo unloads on the entire enemy team. Lower per-target damage but hits everyone. Rapid fire, no aiming, pure suppression.
- *Thought: "Everyone gets one!"*

### Loaded Chamber
Every fourth skill Renzo uses is supercharged — guaranteed critical hit at +50% damage. The counter is visible to the player, creating a minigame of timing action cards to land when the crit is loaded.
- *Thought: "Counting..."* / *"This one's loaded."* (on proc)

### Quick Draw
When Renzo takes damage, there's a 25% chance he fires back instantly — pure reflex, faster than thought. The counter-shot hits before the attacker finishes their animation.
- *Thought: "Too slow."*

### Dead Eye
Renzo's ultimate. One shot, maximum power. The single highest single-target damage in the game. If the target is already wounded (below 30% HP), bonus execution damage. This is why you hire Renzo — for the kill shot.
- *Thought: "One shot. One kill."*

### Lore Relationships

- **Seryn (Storm Ranger)** — Friendly professional respect. Both are ranged killers. She's more precise, he's more flashy. She wins accuracy competitions. He wins speed draws. They argue about which matters more. He's one of the few people she speaks to as an equal. He's one of the few people who can make her almost smile.
- **Kaelen (Arc Strider)** — Speed rivalry. Kaelen is faster in a straight line. Renzo is faster on the draw. They've raced. Results are disputed. Kaelen says he won. Renzo says the bullet got there first. They'll never agree, and neither wants to.
- **Night Venom (Silas)** — Fellow professionals. Renzo kills loud, Silas kills quiet. They've worked the same contracts from different angles. Mutual respect, zero trust. Renzo checks his drinks. Silas watches the windows. Professional habits.
- **Tyrant Overlord** — A regular client. Good pay, clear targets. Renzo doesn't care about conquest. He cares that the payment clears. The Overlord considers Renzo a useful tool. Renzo considers the Overlord a reliable paycheck. Both are correct.
- **Iron Knight (Garen)** — Renzo finds Garen's honor exhausting. Garen finds Renzo's mercenary ethics disgusting. Garen stands between innocents and danger. Renzo stands wherever the money is. They've been on the same battlefield and on opposite sides. Neither lost sleep over it.
- **Dawn Priest (Auryn)** — She tried to save him once — not his body, his soul. He told her souls don't pay rent. She didn't laugh. He respects her conviction the way you respect a wall you can't shoot through. She sees potential in him. He finds that offensive.

## Counters

- **Weak against:** Earth-heavy teams that shake his aim and ground his mobility. Tanks who absorb his burst without dying — Renzo needs kills, not attrition. Dive heroes who close distance before he can stack Loaded Chamber.
- **Strong against:** Low-defense targets who fold to his burst combo (Loaded Chamber crit + Execute or Dead Eye). Slow, immobile teams that can't dodge Explosive Round splash. Any hero who needs time to ramp up — Renzo doesn't give them time.
