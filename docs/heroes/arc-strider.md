# Arc Strider — Kaelen

## Identity

- **Name:** Kaelen
- **Role:** Skirmisher
- **Alignment:** Neutral
- **Order:** Order of the Gale
- **Origin Clan:** Lightning clan
- **Elements:** Wind (+25), Fire (+10), Light (+5)
- **Weaknesses:** Earth (-25)
- **Stats:** 2700 HP / 155 ATK / 115 DEF

## Lore

Kaelen was born into a lightning clan of the highland steppes, where children learn to run before they learn to speak. His clan believed lightning was fire given wings — the purest form of speed made lethal. By the time he was old enough to hold a blade, he could outpace the bolts themselves.

He was recruited into the Order of the Gale at fourteen — the youngest initiate in a generation. The Order is an ancient storm hunter guild that draws the best fighters from clans across the highlands. Their purpose: hunt the storm-born beasts that descend from the tempests, monstrous creatures charged with elemental fury that threaten the lowland settlements.

Kaelen rose through the ranks on instinct and speed. He doesn't plan — he reacts. He doesn't track — he intercepts. His philosophy is simple: *strike first, strike fastest*. If the prey sees you coming, you're already too slow. He channels raw lightning through his body, closing distance in a flash, his blade already cutting before the thunder catches up.

Now he stands as one of two candidates for the Order's next leader. His rival is Seryn, a wind-reader from a different clan. She thinks he's reckless. He thinks she's slow. Neither is wrong — and neither will yield.

## Personality

Impulsive, confident, electric energy. Speaks in short bursts like his fighting style. Competitive but not malicious — he respects strength and speed above all else. Grins in the middle of a fight. Hates waiting. Hates being grounded.

### Arc Slash
Kaelen's bread and butter — a fast wind-charged blade strike at his primary target. The fastest cast time in his kit, matching his philosophy of constant pressure.
- *Thought: "Too slow, {target}!"*

### Thunder Step
Blinks to his target in a crack of lightning and delivers a stunning strike. Short stun gives him tempo advantage.
- *Thought: "Blink and you're done."*

### Storm Pulse
Releases a pulse of lightning around himself, hitting all enemies. His only AoE — used when surrounded or to finish weakened targets.
- *Thought: "The storm is EVERYWHERE!"*

### Static Charge
Every third skill he uses is supercharged. Encourages aggressive play — the more he attacks, the harder he hits. Rewards his "keep moving, keep striking" philosophy.
- *Thought: "Charge building..."* / *"OVERLOADED!"* (on proc)

### Lightning Reflexes
Pure speed — 20% chance to completely dodge an incoming attack. Fits his "faster than sight" identity.
- *Thought: "Missed me."*

### Tempest Surge
Kaelen becomes pure lightning, striking each enemy in rapid sequence with massive damage and a stun. The ultimate expression of his speed — he's everywhere at once.
- *Thought: "You can't outrun lightning!"*
- *Visibility: always (charge bar shown)*

### Lore Relationships

- **Seryn (Storm Ranger)** — His rival in the Order of the Gale. They compete for the rank of next leader. He thinks she's too cautious. She thinks he'll get himself killed. Underneath the rivalry, mutual respect — neither would let the other die.
- **Flame Warlock (Malachar)** — Kaelen sees fire as lightning's slower cousin. He tolerates Malachar's chaos because it creates openings. Malachar finds Kaelen amusing — a mortal trying to be as fast as flame.
- **Wind Monk** — Fellow wind user. The Monk's philosophy of flow appeals to Kaelen more than he admits, though he finds meditation boring.
- **Earth Warden** — Kaelen's worst nightmare. Everything that grounds him, slows him, traps him.
- **Night Venom** — Respects the assassin's speed and precision. A hunter recognizes another hunter.

## Counters

- **Weak against:** Earth-heavy teams ground him, removing his dodge and slowing his cooldowns. Tanks with high defense (Iron Knight, Earth Warden) absorb his fast but moderate-damage strikes.
- **Strong against:** Low-defense, slow heroes who can't handle his speed. His stuns disrupt casters mid-channel. Glass cannons fold to his burst combo (Thunder Step into Static Charge proc).
