# Auryn, the Dawn Priest

## Lore

Auryn didn't choose the light — the light chose her before she was born. She is the last of the Solari bloodline, a sacred lineage that has led the holy order for centuries. Each generation produced one prophet, one voice of the light. Auryn is the final one. There will be no successor.

She has carried this weight since childhood. Revered by thousands, understood by no one. The order sees a prophet. Aldric sees a leader worth dying for. Only Garen sees a person — a tired woman carrying a burden she never asked for. That's why she trusts him. He doesn't worship her. He just stands in front of her.

Her power is immense but not violent. The light flows through her to mend, purify, and protect. She can destroy — but every time she does, something in her dims. She heals because it's who she is. She fights only because the light demands it.

When Garen's kingdom fell, it was Auryn who opened the order's gates to the survivors. She didn't do it for strategy or politics. She did it because suffering is the one thing she cannot ignore. Garen never thanked her. She never needed him to.

## Personality

Warm but distant. She speaks with a calm that borders on otherworldly — not cold, but untouchable. She cares deeply but has spent her entire life being treated as something more than human, and it shows. She is kind to everyone and close to almost no one.

The only people who see past the prophet are Garen, who treats her like a person, and Aldric, who sees the weight she carries even if he'd never let her carry it alone.

## Lore Relationships

- **Aldric (Arcane Paladin)** — Her sworn protector. He was raised to serve her, and he has never once questioned that purpose. She sometimes wishes he would. Not because she doubts him — because she wonders if he chose this, or if the light chose it for him. She would never ask. The answer might break them both.
- **Garen (Iron Knight)** — The only person who sees her as a person, not a prophet. He doesn't kneel. He doesn't pray. He just stands in front of her and bleeds so she doesn't have to. She opened her gates to his survivors because she couldn't bear their suffering. He never thanked her. She never needed him to. That's why he's the one she trusts.
- **Princess Emberheart** — A kindred spirit in nobility. Emberheart carries her own weight — a princess who fights instead of hiding. Auryn respects that more than she can say. Two women leading from the front.
- **Necromancer** — The opposite of everything she is. He reaches into death. She reaches into light. She doesn't hate him — she grieves for him. He was someone's child once. Now he's something else.
- **Demon Empress** — Darkness given a throne. Auryn feels the Empress's shadow like a wound. Being near her physically hurts. She would purify her if she could. She can't. That haunts her.
- **Flame Warlock (Malachar)** — She could have saved him once, when the fire was young and his mind was still his own. She didn't reach him in time. Now the fire is all that's left. She doesn't fight Malachar with anger. She fights him with grief.
