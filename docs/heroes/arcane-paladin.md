# Aldric, the Arcane Paladin

## Lore

Aldric is the sword and shield of the Dawn Priest. He was born into the holy order she leads — raised from boyhood to serve the light and protect its voice. The Dawn Priest is that voice. Where she speaks, he stands guard. Where she heals, he holds the line. His faith isn't abstract — it has a face, and he has sworn his life to it.

He rose through the order not through ambition, but through unwavering conviction. Every trial, every battle, every test of faith — Aldric passed without hesitation. Not because he's mindless, but because every time the Dawn Priest spoke, she was right. His certainty isn't blind. It's earned.

His shadow resistance doesn't come from wielding darkness — it comes from being so steeped in light that shadow slides off him like water off stone. He has faced creatures of the abyss and walked through their corruption unchanged. The shadow affinity in his soul is a wall, not a door.

Fire is his weakness. Raw heat agitates the arcane light he channels, destabilizing his holy barriers. Wind-laced strikes slip past the weight of his conviction. He knows this. He advances anyway.

He met Garen when the order took in survivors of the fallen kingdom. Two protectors from different worlds — one serves faith, the other serves guilt. They don't need to explain themselves to each other. They just stand side by side and hold.

## Personality

Absolute, calm, immovable. Speaks with quiet certainty — not arrogance, but the steady voice of someone who has never needed to doubt. He doesn't preach. He doesn't lecture. He simply is. Some find his conviction inspiring. Others find it unnerving. He treats both reactions the same way: with patience.

The only crack in his composure is when the Dawn Priest is threatened. Then the calm breaks, and something ancient and furious rises in its place.

## Lore Relationships

- **Dawn Priest** — She is his purpose. He was raised to serve the light, and she is the light's voice. Not devotion born of romance — devotion born of absolute faith. He would walk into the abyss if she asked. She would never ask, and that's why he trusts her.
- **Garen (Iron Knight)** — Brothers in protection. Garen protects out of guilt, Aldric out of faith. They met when Aldric's order took in Garen's survivors. They don't talk about why they fight. They just fight. Side by side.
- **Princess Emberheart** — He respects her nobility and her fire. Her flame is controlled, purposeful — the opposite of Malachar's chaos. He'd guard her if the Dawn Priest asked him to.
- **Necromancer** — A dark mirror. The Necromancer reached into death the way Aldric reaches into light. Same devotion, opposite direction. Aldric doesn't hate him. He pities him. That's worse.
- **Demon Empress** — Her shadow calls to weak men. Aldric is not weak. Her presence is a test he has passed a hundred times. She finds his immunity infuriating.
- **Flame Warlock (Malachar)** — Chaos without purpose. Fire without duty. Aldric sees Malachar as a tragedy — brilliant potential consumed by madness. He would have tried to save him once. Not anymore.
