# Iron Knight — Garen

## Identity

- **Name:** Garen
- **Role:** Heavy Tank
- **Alignment:** Rightful
- **Elements:** Earth (+30), Ice (+10), Light (+10)
- **Weaknesses:** Fire (-15), Wind (-20)
- **Stats:** 3500 HP / 130 ATK / 180 DEF

## Lore

Garen was the shield-captain of a kingdom that no longer exists. When the kingdom fell — overwhelmed by shadow and hellfire — he was the last man standing at the gate, holding the line while civilians fled. He held it for three days. When the walls finally crumbled, there was no one left to protect, and no kingdom left to serve.

Now he fights without a lord, without a banner, but the oath still burns. He swore to protect, and an oath doesn't die just because the kingdom did. He attaches himself to whoever needs a shield — not for coin, not for glory, but because standing between the vulnerable and the violent is the only thing that quiets the guilt of being the one who survived.

He's old for a fighter. His armor is dented, repaired a hundred times. He doesn't talk about the kingdom. He doesn't need to be thanked. He just needs someone to stand in front of.

## Personality

Disciplined, blunt, protective. Speaks like a field order and measures worth in steadiness under pressure. Recklessness offends him more than cowardice. He doesn't waste words. He doesn't waste movement. Everything he does is in service of the line holding.

### Shield Bash
A heavy strike with the edge of his shield. Low damage, but the stun forces enemies to deal with him.
- *Thought: "Stand down, {target}."*

### Hold the Line
Garen raises his shield high and his allies feel the weight of his oath. Everyone gets tougher.
- *Thought: "Behind me. All of you."*

### Bulwark
Garen passively absorbs a portion of damage meant for his allies. He doesn't need to be asked. He just steps in front.
- *Thought: "I'll take that hit."*

### Retribution
Steel remembers who struck it. 40% chance to reflect damage back as light-infused retaliation.
- *Thought: "Steel remembers."*

### Last Stand
When Garen is nearly dead, he refuses to fall. A hidden surge of willpower grants him a massive shield and defense buff. He held a gate for three days once. He can hold a little longer.
- *Thought: "I will NOT fall."*

### Vow of Two Shields
Hidden skill. Only exists when Arcane Paladin is on the team. When either protector drops below 25% HP, the other instantly shields them. Two oaths, stronger together.
- *Garen's thought: "We hold together."*
- *Arcane Paladin's thought: "The vow stands."*

### Unbreakable Oath
Garen's ultimate. Charges as he takes damage — the more he suffers, the closer he gets to his most powerful moment. When full, he shields and cleanses the entire team, becoming nearly unkillable himself.
- *Thought: "By steel and light — NONE shall pass!"*

**Alignment: Rightful** — Garen fights for duty and protection. He will not ally with chaotic or dark heroes.

### Lore Relationships

- **Arcane Paladin** — The closest thing Garen has to an equal. Both are protectors, both carry oaths. Garen distrusts the shadow in the Paladin's magic but respects the discipline that holds it in check. They fight as one wall.
- **Dawn Priest** — The closest thing Garen has to a friend. Steel and faith. The Priest doesn't try to fix him or ask about the kingdom. He just stands behind the shield and heals.
- **Princess Emberheart** — She reminds him of the kingdom he lost. Her fire should repel him, but her nobility overrides it. He'd die for her without hesitation, and she doesn't even know why.
- **Flame Warlock (Malachar)** — Fire destroyed his kingdom. Malachar is everything Garen despises — power without duty, destruction without purpose. Being on the same field makes Garen's armor literally burn.
- **Tyrant Overlord** — The kind of warlord who destroyed Garen's kingdom. Pure hatred. Garen would break formation just to reach him.
- **Earth Warden** — A kindred guardian. Older, less human, but Garen understands the Warden's purpose. Two mountains on the same side.

## Counters

- **Weak against:** Sustained fire damage cooks him inside his armor. Wind/lightning bypasses his heavy defense. Heroes who ignore tanks and dive his backline make his protection useless.
- **Strong against:** Burst damage dealers who can't get through his layered shields. Melee heroes who have to fight through his stuns, taunts, and Retribution. Any team that focuses him feeds his ultimate charge.
